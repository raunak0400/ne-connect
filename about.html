Our Mission

Simplifying the Move to Your New City

NE Connect was born out of a real need felt by thousands of students and young professionals across Assam, Meghalaya, Manipur, Nagaland, Mizoram, and the other north-eastern states.

Moving to a new city for work is exciting — but finding a job and a safe place to stay simultaneously is overwhelming. We built one platform to solve both problems at once.

The Problem & Our Solution

The Challenge

  • North-east India has a large population facing significant employment challenges
  • Students and workers struggle to find reliable housing when relocating to cities like Guwahati
  • Job seekers lack access to accurate information about available positions
  • Difficulties finding suitable housing near workplaces
  • High competition leads to exploitation and uncertain living conditions
  • No single trusted platform exists for both needs in the region

Our Solution

  • Connect job seekers with companies that have listed verified vacancies
  • Provide safe, affordable, convenient housing near listed companies
  • Help north-east natives find stable employment
  • Ensure a reliable place to stay during their city transition
  • Simplify relocation with a single, trusted platform
  • Improve overall quality of life for north-east's workforce
